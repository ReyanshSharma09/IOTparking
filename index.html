❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before reserving

What happens if traffic delays me? +
Our system integrates with live traffic data. If there's a major traffic incident on your route, you may be eligible for an automatic extension. Priority and VIP tiers also include grace periods for minor delays.
How do I check in? +
Check-in is automatic when you enter the 500m geo-fence around the parking location. You can also manually check-in using the QR code at the entrance or through the app.
Can I extend my reservation? +
Yes! You can extend your parking duration from the app. Extensions are charged at the hourly rate. The arrival time window cannot be extended once the timer starts, except for verified traffic incidents.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. A valid payment method must be on file to make a reservation, as penalties are automatically charged.
How are penalties charged? +
Penalties are automatically charged to your payment method on file when a reservation expires without check-in. You'll receive an email notification with details. You can appeal penalties within 24 hours if you have a valid reason.
Is my data secure? +
Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption for all transactions and data. Your location is only tracked during active reservations and is never shared with third parties. View our Privacy Policy for details.
