Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about ParkSmart Pro

❓ What happens if I'm late?
+
If you arrive late, a penalty fee (20-50% of booking) applies. After the deadline, your reservation is automatically released to the next user. No-shows lose the full reservation fee.
💰 Can I cancel and get a refund?
+
Yes! Standard tier offers 70% refund if cancelled within 30 minutes. Priority and VIP tiers offer better cancellation terms — VIP allows free cancellation up to 5 minutes before arrival.
📍 How does auto check-in work?
+
Our system uses GPS geo-fencing. When you enter a 500m radius of your parking spot, you're automatically checked in. The gate opens via license plate recognition or QR code as backup.
🎯 Which tier should I choose?
+
Standard: Best for casual users with reliable timing. Priority: Recommended for most users — better spots + grace period. VIP: Perfect for business meetings, events, or when timing is uncertain.
🔮 What is "predictive availability"?
+
Our AI analyzes historical data, current occupancy, and demand patterns to predict when spots will open. You'll see notes like "5 spots opening in 15 mins" or "Lot will be full by 2 PM."
🏢 Can I list my parking lot?
+
Absolutely! Visit our pricing section to choose a plan. We offer Starter ($49/mo), Professional ($149/mo), and Enterprise (custom) plans with full analytics dashboards and dynamic pricing tools.
